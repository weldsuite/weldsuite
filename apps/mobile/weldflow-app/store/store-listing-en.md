## Full description (both, max 4000 chars)
WeldFlow is the mobile companion for WeldSuite project management. Pull up your projects, scan through what's assigned to you, and move tasks forward without opening a laptop.

Built for project managers, team leads, and anyone juggling work across multiple projects, WeldFlow gives you the fastest path to the one task you actually need to update right now.

FEATURES
- Projects list with status, progress, and team counts at a glance
- Task list with status, priority, due date, and overdue warnings
- One-tap status updates, change a task from To Do to In Progress in a single gesture
- My Tasks dashboard, see every task assigned to you across all projects, filtered by status
- Smart filters, project status (Planning, Active, On Hold, Completed) and task status
- Push notifications, get pinged when a task is assigned to you or updated
- Light and dark mode, synced with your system settings
- Secure sign-in with your WeldSuite account (Clerk-powered authentication)
- Enterprise workspace support, switch between workspaces from Settings

REQUIREMENTS
- An active WeldSuite workspace (sign up at weldsuite.com)
- Projects and tasks you're a member of

WeldFlow is the mobile-first extension of the WeldSuite platform. All changes you make in the app sync instantly with the web version at app.weldsuite.org.
